Elastane Another name for spandex, this synthetic fiber is known for its exceptional elasticity and durability. TENCEL™ TENCEL™ fibers are made of natural cellulose derived from sustainably harvested wood pulp from renewable tree farms. These modern, eco-friendly fibers are also known as TENCEL™ Lyocell and TENCEL™ Modal. They help to make buttery-soft, smooth, strong fabrics with a luxurious hand and elegant drape, yet can be highly absorbent and versatile in other fabrics. TENCEL™ Lyocell and Modal fibers are widely used in clothing and home textiles, from denim, chino and casual attire to bath towels and bed linens.
